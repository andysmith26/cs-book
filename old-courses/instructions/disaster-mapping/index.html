Introduction

Disaster relief teams are often missing current and accurate maps of the disaster zones where they work. In recent years volunteers have developed a technology platform that allows anyone around the world to improve the maps that that are used in the immediate aftermath of a disaster.

Watch the following short video to learn a bit more.

Goals

  • learn what OSM is
  • learn how to edit OSM
  • help disaster relief teams by making edits to OSM

Create an OSM Account

  1. Go to the signup page linked here.
  2. Enter the requested info except 'password' and 'confirm password'.
  3. Click "Alternatively, use a third party to login"
  4. Click the "None" box and change it to "Google".
  5. Still do not enter a password. Click "Sign Up".
